# All Steps of This App (Brief)

## Paper 2 – TyG Research Pipeline (Main workflow)

1. **Extract** – Upload or paste PDFs; OCR or LLM extract fills patient rows (ID, name, age, sex, glucose, TG, HDL, waist).
2. **Verify** – Review and edit extracted data in the verification table; fix any misreads.
3. **Edit Waist** – Adjust waist values per patient.
4. **Analyze** – View TyG and other metabolic stats/charts from verified data.
5. **Write JCDR** – Generate JCDR-format manuscript from the same patient set.

## Other routes

6. **Step 6** – Journal submission (format and submit to journal).
7. **Step 7** – Quality assurance (grammar, plagiarism, journal formatting).
8. **Step 8** – Social media content from manuscript (Twitter, LinkedIn, infographic).
9. **Step 9** – MCQs and Gamma slides for education.
10. **Step 10** – (If present) – as per app routes.
11. **Step 11** – Content amplification (blog, YouTube, social templates).
12. **Step 12** – **Neutral content**: topic + source (upload/paste/scratch) → language + formats → generate blog/Twitter/handout/etc.; optional **PubMed Evidence (MCT)** and **Nutrition Bot** for evidence-based or food content.
13. **Step 13** – (If present) – as per app routes.

## Supporting flows

- **Published / Submitted** – View Paper 1 (published) and Paper 2 (submitted).
- **Content Ops** – Batch upload, content queue, Google Sheet sync, social tracker.
- **Admin – Collaboration** – View and manage collaboration submissions.

## In one line

**Extract → Verify → Waist → Analyze → Write JCDR** is the core pipeline; Steps 6–11 handle submission, QA, social, education, and amplification; **Step 12** is the standalone neutral content + PubMed/Nutrition Bot tool.
